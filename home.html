Here, amidst rolling hills and vibrant greenery, a unique practice called "Gukaniza urukana" has been passed down through generations, preserving the essence of community and simplicity.

OUR VISION

Ngabo Karegeya is a charismatic and entrepreneurial individual from the rural region of Bigogwe in Rwanda. He developed a deep passion for cows and the natural beauty of his home, the green hills of Bigogwe. Growing up in a pastoral community, Ngabo likely learned the traditional ways of herding and caring for cows from an early age.

WELCOME TO SMART SWAMP

Welcome to SMART SWAP, a leading secondary institution in the heart of Rwanda dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and integrity. Founded on the principle that education is the ultimate engine of transformation, our school provides a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to reach their full potential and become the visionary leaders of tomorrow.
